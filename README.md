# Scientific Computing & Physical Modeling 💻🔬

## 🎯 Purpose
This repository serves as a technical portfolio of computational algorithms developed during my academic career at the **University of São Paulo (USP)** between 2019 and 2023. It bridges the gap between theoretical physics and computational science, focusing on numerical methods, statistical fitting, and the simulation of physical systems.

## 📂 Core Content

### 1. Experimental Physics (CERN ROOT / C++)
Automated analysis of modern physics experiments using statistical inference.
* **Peak Fitting:** Gaussian modeling for Mercury spectrometer calibration.
* **Fundamental Constants:** Linear regression for Rydberg Constant determination.
* **Radiation Interaction:** Beer-Lambert Law modeling for X-ray attenuation.

### 2. Fluid Mechanics (MATLAB)
Numerical solutions for potential flow theory.
* **Flow Simulation:** Modeling of Rankine Half-Bodies and streamlines.

### 3. Astroparticle Physics (Scientific Initiation - IC)
Computational methods applied to Dark Matter research and Galactic Dynamics.
* **Capture Rates:** Numerical integration for WIMP capture in celestial bodies.
* **Rotation Curves:** Mathematical modeling of galactic density profiles (NFW).

## 🛠️ Technical Skills
* **Statistical Modeling:** Chi-square minimization and non-linear least squares.
* **Numerical Analysis:** Integration, root-finding, and differential equations.
* **Scientific Tools:** Advanced proficiency in CERN ROOT, MATLAB, and C++.
